LEGAL SERVICES

Welcome to New York City! Adjusting to life in the big city can be intimidating, especially when it comes to legal matters. But don't worry, we're here to help you navigate through it all.

In this guide, we'll share tips and resources just for newcomers like you. Whether you're facing immigration issues, need help with citizenship applications, or are seeking legal advice for any other matter, we've got what you need to succeed.

Free Immigration Legal Help

ActionNYC provides free and safe immigration legal help through trusted community organizations and attorneys.

To make an appointment, call 1-800-354-0365 or 311 and say "ActionNYC".

Lawyer Finder Service

The City Bar's Legal Referral Service provides referrals to screened lawyers for various legal matters, including immigration.

Call (212) 626-7373 (English) or (212) 626-7374 (Spanish).

Multilingual Information

New York State New Americans Hotline offers free, multilingual, and confidential information on immigration benefits and services.

Call (800) 566-7636, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 8 PM.

Immigration Services Fraud

Beware of fraudulent immigration service providers. Seek legal help only from trusted, licensed attorneys or accredited representatives.

For questions about immigration fraud, call the New Americans Hotline at 1-800-566-7636, Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 8 PM.

Citizenship Application Support

NYCitizenship offers free legal assistance with citizenship applications and financial counseling at select public library branches.

To make an appointment, call 311 and say "citizenship appointment" or call (212) 514-4277.
